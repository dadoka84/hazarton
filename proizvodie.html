About the product and the production

The patentee of the Bosnian stylish furniture Hazarton – Haznadarevic design is mr. Haris Haznadarevic. The style has evolved from the cultures that existed in Bosnia for over 500 years; thus, besides the top quality of production, there is a unique and new design as well.

Thanks to the tradition of many years, our production capacities and an expert production team which consists of carpenters, engravers, upholsterers, lacquerers, mosaic woodworkers, cutters and artists, we can gladly point out that our furniture is a pure Bosnian product.

Every piece of our furniture is made from natural wood of high quality (oak-tree, walnut-tree, cherry-tree, beech-tree, maple-tree). All carvings and engravings are done manually.

Our stylish furniture is characterised by a unique design, high quality, absolute functionality and very suitable prices, both for house furniture and office furniture. We must also point out that Hazarton has until now given the best solution for stylish pull-out suits.

We give permanent guarantee for all our products.
